Vegan Black Bean Tacos

These easy and delicious vegan tacos are made with seasoned black beans, fresh vegetables, and a tangy lime dressing. A perfect plant-based meal that’s full of flavor!

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (400g) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the minced garlic, ground cumin, chili powder, salt, and black pepper. Cook for another minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the black beans and cook for 5-7 minutes, until heated through and slightly softened. Mash some of the beans with the back of a spoon for a creamier texture.
  4. Warm the corn tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave until pliable.
  5. Assemble the tacos by adding the black bean mixture to each tortilla. Top with shredded lettuce, avocado slices, cherry tomatoes, and fresh cilantro.
  6. Squeeze lime wedges over the tacos for a burst of fresh flavor. Serve immediately.

Enjoy your flavorful and healthy Vegan Black Bean Tacos!
