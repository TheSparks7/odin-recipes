Mediterranean Chickpea Salad

A refreshing and healthy salad packed with chickpeas, fresh vegetables, herbs, and a tangy lemon dressing. Perfect for a quick meal or side dish!

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (400g) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chickpeas, cucumber, bell pepper, red onion, cherry tomatoes, feta cheese, and parsley.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, lemon juice, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  4. Adjust the seasoning if needed. Serve immediately or refrigerate for 1-2 hours for the flavors to meld together.

Enjoy your fresh and delicious Mediterranean Chickpea Salad!
