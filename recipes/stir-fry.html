Chicken Teriyaki Stir-Fry

A quick and flavorful stir-fry made with tender chicken, fresh vegetables, and a delicious teriyaki sauce. Perfect for a weeknight dinner!

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Sesame seeds for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix the soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, cornstarch, and water to make the teriyaki sauce. Set aside.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the chicken slices and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the garlic, broccoli, bell pepper, and carrot. Stir-fry the vegetables until tender-crisp, about 5 minutes.
  4. Return the chicken to the skillet. Pour the teriyaki sauce over the chicken and vegetables, stirring until everything is evenly coated and the sauce has thickened.
  5. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the sauce is glossy and coats the stir-fry well.
  6. Garnish with sesame seeds if desired. Serve hot over steamed rice or noodles.

Enjoy your savory and delicious Chicken Teriyaki Stir-Fry!
