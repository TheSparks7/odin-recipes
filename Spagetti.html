Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. A quick and delicious meal!

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the pancetta and cook until crispy, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add minced garlic to the skillet and saut√© for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix in grated Parmesan cheese. Set aside.
  5. Drain the pasta, reserving a cup of pasta water. Add the pasta to the skillet with pancetta and garlic.
  6. Remove the skillet from heat and quickly add the egg and cheese mixture, tossing to coat the pasta evenly.
  7. If needed, add some reserved pasta water to adjust the consistency of the sauce.
  8. Season with salt and black pepper. Serve immediately with extra grated Parmesan cheese on top.

And There you have it. Your tasty meal ready to be eaten!
